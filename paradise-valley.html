Loan Structures

What Financing Looks Like in Paradise Valley

Almost everything in Paradise Valley is jumbo territory. The 2026 conforming limit for Maricopa County is $806,500 — a number that barely registers in this market. Typical transactions run $1.5M to $8M and involve financing structures that require a lender who knows these products cold.

Product Rate Type Typical Use Case
30yr Jumbo Fixed Fixed Long-term owners, buyers prioritizing rate certainty over initial payment level
10/1 ARM Adjustable Buyers planning to move or upgrade in 7–10 years; lower initial rate reduces carrying cost
Interest-Only ARM Adjustable Cash-flow management for high-net-worth buyers with complex asset allocation
Asset Depletion Fixed or ARM HNW buyers qualifying via liquid assets rather than traditional W-2 salary income

Down payment expectations in PV run 20% to 30% — sellers and their agents review this as part of evaluating competing offers. Reserve requirements are also higher: PV sellers sometimes request proof of 12 to 24 months of PITIA in liquid assets before accepting an offer. We front-load verification so you are never caught flat-footed when the window opens.
