## Design Context

### Users
Logan Loans serves Arizona and Southern California homebuyers, move-up buyers, investors, self-employed borrowers, and referral partners. The priority audience for this pass is Arizona real estate professionals who need a lender they can confidently introduce to clients, plus buyers who arrive from those referrals and need fast clarity before making an offer.

### Brand Personality
Calm, precise, personal, and premium. The site should feel like an experienced mortgage advisor who picks up the phone, explains the math, protects the agent-client relationship, and keeps deals moving without hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined financial editorial with warm Arizona cues: cream paper, deep evergreen, sage, restrained apricot, crisp typography, generous whitespace, and polished motion. Avoid generic admin/SaaS patterns, glass-heavy decoration, bouncy motion, emoji-driven UI, sterile bank templates, and overly casual broker marketing.

### Design Principles
- Evidence stays close to each decision: NMLS, reviews, timelines, service area, and process proof should appear where trust is being requested.
- Realtor intent is a first-class path, not a hidden secondary page. Internal links should make “Arizona Realtor mortgage partner” easy for users and crawlers to discover.
- Motion should feel expensive: quiet entrances, clear feedback, no bounce or elastic curves, no constant decoration, and full reduced-motion support.
- Copy should be direct, specific, and useful. Prefer deal mechanics, communication promises, and qualification clarity over generic “dream home” language.
- SEO and AI-search readiness come from crawlable text, consistent schema that matches visible content, strong internal links, page speed, and people-first expertise.
